About Me

Hi, I'm Hoagie. Well at least thats what my friends call me. I have been on a constant journey of learning new skills and hobbies. Some topics include coding, music production and sleight of hand, but that is just scratching the surface. I have put countless hours into these topics and thought it would be best to share this with others. Thus I started Big Hoagie Productions which focuses on music production but with growth and development of the community I intend on sharing everything I have to offer. Plus who hasn't heard the phrase "Knowledge is Power". I want to empower everyone around me and hope you will join me on the journey. Thanks again for visiting the site and all of your support.
